TributeDAO Framework

A new modular DAO framework inspired by the Moloch smart contracts.

Get StartedLaunch your DAO Tutorial

Modular

Designed to make DAO's easy to assemble, like lego blocks, utilizing a narrowly-defined, tested, and extensible set smart contracts.

Low Cost

TributeDAO Framework enables low cost deployments due to its design and architecture based on Clone Factory pattern.

Extensible

Custom extensions isolate the complexity of DAO core contracts and enable enhancements of it with new features.
